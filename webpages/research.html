Research

The main content on our website's homepage website is inspired from the template of Wix's website. The position of the text, styles of the navigation bar, and buttons has been changed.

The body part of our homepage is also inspired from Wix's website template's. We did some changes to text, pictures and funtionalities of the images.

"This section of our 'About Us' page is inspired by the 'About Us' page on eSewa's website. We made some changes to the container in the team members' profiles, such as adding a subtle box shadow that appears when hovering over a member's profile."

The contact form on our website is inspired by Sleeknote's design. We made modifications to the labels, inputs, text areas, and image alignment. Additionally, we introduced a style feature that causes the submit button to change its background color on hover.

Our website's product page has drawn inspiration from Daraz's product page design. We've made specific modifications to enhance the layout of the product's cart and the filters, aiming to improve the overall user experience.

Our website's blog page has been inspired by a template from Wixx's website. We've carefully customized elements, including refining the text, enhancing the hover effects, and adjusting the background color of the buttons.
